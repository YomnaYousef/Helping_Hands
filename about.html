About Us


Helping Hands is platform that connects donors with people or organizations in need. We provide a convenient and efficient way for people to donate their unused or unwanted items to those who could benefit from them.
This can include food, clothing, household goods, and other items that are in good condition but no longer needed by the donor. The website can provide a platform for donors to list the items they want to donate, and for recipients to browse and request the items they need.
